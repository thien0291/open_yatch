## 2. Cost Drivers
* **Resin Type**: Poly vs. Vinylester (VE is 2x price but better osmosis protection).
* **Construction Method**: Hand lay-up (Labor intensive, cheaper tooling) vs. Infusion (Consumables cost, lower weight).
* **Core Material**: Balsa (Cheap, risk of rot) vs. PVC Foam (Expensive, rot-proof).

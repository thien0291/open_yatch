# WORK INSTRUCTION: Handover & Delivery

**ID:** BLD-05-03
**Role:** Project Manager
**Time Est:** 1 Day
**Prerequisites:** Sea Trials Passed, Boat Cleaned.

---

## 1. OBJECTIVE
Give the keys to the owner and get paid.

---

## 2. PREPARATION

### The "Gift Box"
Prepare a box containing:
- [ ] 2 sets of Keys (Ignition, Door, Locker).
- [ ] Floating Keychains.
- [ ] Owner's Manual (Binder with all manuals).
- [ ] Warranty Certificates.
- [ ] Registration Papers.

---

## 3. STEP-BY-STEP INSTRUCTIONS

### STEP 1: The Walkthrough
**Worker:** PM + Owner
**Action:**
Show them how it works.

**Instruction:**
1.  **Start/Stop**: Have the Owner start the engine.
2.  **Breakers**: Show where the Main Switch is.
3.  **Toilets**: TEACH THEM how to pump the toilet.
    *   *Rule*: "If you haven't eaten it, don't flush it."
4.  **Seacocks**: Show where every hull valve is. Label them.

### STEP 2: The Snag List
**Worker:** PM
**Action:**
Manage expectations.

**Instruction:**
1.  **Inspect**: Walk with the owner. Look for scratches, stiff hinges, etc.
2.  **List**: Write down everything they are unhappy with.
3.  **Agree**:
    *   *Critical*: Fix before leaving.
    *   *Minor*: Fix at 1-month service.
    *   **Sign**: Both parties sign the list.

### STEP 3: The Champagne
**Worker:** All
**Action:**
Celebrate.

**Instruction:**
1.  Hand over the keys.
2.  Shake hands.
3.  Take a photo.

---

## 4. QA CHECKLIST (Final)

*   [ ] **Cleanliness**: Is the bilge clean? (Owners look there first).
*   [ ] **Fuel**: Is the tank full? (Nice gesture).
*   [ ] **Fridge**: Is it cold?

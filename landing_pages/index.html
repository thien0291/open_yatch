Where Safety Meets
Sublime Design

The Open Yacht project is not merely a vessel; it is a statement of intent. Engineered in Vietnam, it leverages the precision of the BMAD (Build, Measure, Assess, Design) methodology to achieve a level of finish and safety rarely seen in its class.

From the panoramic glazing that dissolves the boundary between guest and ocean, to the AC-first electrical architecture providing silent, robust power – every detail is calculated.
